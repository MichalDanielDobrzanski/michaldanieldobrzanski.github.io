Support

If something's wrong, write — we read every message.

The fastest path

Email hello@pray.app. Include your platform (iOS / Android), the app version (Settings → About), and what you saw. A screenshot helps if you have one.

If you're in crisis

If you're thinking about hurting yourself, please reach out to people trained to help — they answer at any hour and they will not judge you.

  • United States: dial or text 988 for the Suicide & Crisis Lifeline.
  • United Kingdom & ROI: dial 116 123 for Samaritans.
  • International: the directory at findahelpline.com lists hotlines by country.

Common questions

My prayer didn't sound personal

The more specific your prompt — a name, a relationship, the situation — the more the prayer can hold. "Pray for my mom" gives less to work with than "Pray for my mom Linda, who's recovering from surgery."

I want a refund

Apple and Google handle all subscription billing, so refunds go through them, not us. Open the App Store / Play Store, find your subscription, and request a refund there.

I want to delete everything

Settings → Account → Delete account. This wipes your library, intentions, and account row permanently. We cannot recover it.
