What Lumen is

Lumen is a personal devotional companion. It uses an AI model to draft a prayer text shaped around the prompt, intentions, and tone you provide, then streams that text as spoken audio. The output is generated content — it is not pastoral counsel, theological instruction, or a substitute for the sacraments, a priest, a therapist, or a doctor.

Acceptable use

  • Do not attempt to extract, mirror, or scrape the service. The free tier and rate limits exist so the app stays sustainable.
  • Do not use Lumen to generate content for or about another identifiable person who has not consented.

Crisis content

If you describe a thought of suicide or self-harm, the app will not produce a prayer. It will display the phone numbers of crisis hotlines in your region instead. Please use them — those people answer at any hour and they will not judge you.

Generated content disclaimer

The prayer texts are produced by an AI model. They are written to reflect the Catholic tradition but can contain mistakes, awkward phrasing, or — rarely — content that lands wrongly for you. Skip, regenerate, or delete anything that doesn't sit right. If something genuinely concerning appears, please email us so we can improve the safety pipeline.
